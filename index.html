Arrival Day ✈️

📍 Haneda → Disney Area🏨 Hilton Tokyo Bay
Settle In
14:00–17:00
✈️ Start at Haneda Airport → Arrive at Hilton Tokyo Bay
Immigration, bags, IC cards, pocket Wi‑Fi/SIM, then transfer to the hotel. Fastest public-transit option: Keikyu Line / Tokyo Monorail into central Tokyo, then JR Keiyo Line to Maihama, then Disney Resort Line or taxi to Hilton Tokyo Bay. Typical travel time: ~60–90 min after airport formalities. Easier option with kids + luggage: Airport Limousine Bus direct to the Disney Resort area, usually ~45–70 min depending on traffic.
Kids under 6 ride free on trains with an adult. Buy children’s IC cards for ages 6–11.
17:30–19:30
🍽️ Dinner at Ikspiari Mall
Commute from hotel: Disney Resort Line or short taxi / hotel shuttle to Maihama area, ~10–15 min door to door. Options: Ikspiari food court, pizza, curry, Rainforest Cafe, or easy konbini dinner back at the hotel.
Keep dinner flexible and light — this is a jet-lag recovery night.
19:30–20:00
🏨 Return to Hilton Tokyo Bay
From Ikspiari / Maihama Station, ride the Disney Resort Line back toward Bayside Station or take a short taxi. Typical commute: ~10–15 min.
